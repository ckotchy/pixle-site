About Us

We are a team of two creators who were sick and tired of all the new NFT projects coming out. Everything was either another ape project or another cartoon animal project, we wanted to return to simplicity with Pixle NFT Society. Instead of waiting around for a project we would want to invest in, we decided to take matters into our own hands and create the project ourselves. We have big plans for this project !
