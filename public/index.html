About Us

Pixle NFT is the next wave in this ever-changing NFT space. Made intially by a team of two who were sick and tired of seeing animals and uninterested primate clones. We decided to take matters into our own hands, and began scrupulously working on trying to combat this problem that we as a NFT community face. That is when it hit us! Our idea was to strip back all these excessively accessorised and sometimes ridiculous animals to something basic and familiar… Pixels. The idea was staring at us right in the face. We took the building blocks of what NFTs are made of and “zoomed in”. Despite taking the art to its most simplistic and primitive form, we want to be considered as pioneers in the NFT world. We are set to offer as much utility in as many original ways as possible while rewarding our holders. These kinds of utility come in many forms; giveaways, access to the Pixle NFT society, events, Metaverse utility, tokens/staking and much more. Our primary goal is to create a long lasting community that anybody would want to be a part of.
